About Tic Tac Toe

A classic strategy game — simple to learn, endlessly fun to play.

📖What is Tic Tac Toe?

Tic Tac Toe is one of the world's oldest and most beloved two-player games. Players take turns marking spaces on a 3×3 grid — the first to align three of their marks in a row, column, or diagonal wins the round. Sounds simple? The real fun is in the strategy!

⭕

🎮How to Play

1. Start the game — Click "Start Game!" from the home screen. Player X always goes first.

2. Make your move — Click any empty cell on the grid to place your mark (X or O).

3. Take turns — Players alternate every turn. No skipping, no taking it back!

4. Win or Draw — Line up 3 in a row to win! If all 9 cells fill up with no winner, it's a draw.

🏆How to Win

💠3 in a row (horizontal)

💠3 in a column (vertical)

💠3 diagonally

💡Quick Tips

💠Control the center first

💠Watch your opponent's moves
